Travel

With New Zealand being an island nation, the main method to get there is with plane. Singapore airlines have the most flights to New Zealand from Europe, with Qantas being another option, if you care to travel through Australia.

Visa

A visa is not required if you are a citizen of any country in the European union, but you do need one if you’re from Norway. Attached is a link to a website explaining more about New Zealand’s visa policies

Accommodation

New Zealand has no shortage of stunning, beautiful, and unique hotels. I can’t mention them all here, but here are two handpicked suggestions of where to stay during your visit. They also happen to be near some of the attractions mentioned earlier. We have:

  • The Woodlyn Park hotel , home to some of the most unique dwellings I have ever seen, and as a bonus, it's only a short two minutes away from the Waitomo glowworm caves
  • The Mercure Wellington Abel Tasman hotel, with good rooms and good location, being close to the Museum of New Zealand/Te Papa Tongarewa.
